About Life Link

Welcome to Life Link, where our mission is to revolutionize emergency medical services through cutting-edge technology and unparalleled care. Founded in 2010, Life Link was established with a mission to revolutionize emergency medical services, Life Link emerged from a vision to transform the traditional ambulance service model into a more efficient, responsive, and patient-centered system. Our name reflects our commitment to connecting people with life-saving care, bridging gaps between emergency response and immediate medical assistance.

Our Journey

Life Link was established with a commitment to enhancing emergency medical services through innovation and technology. Since our inception, we have focused on integrating advanced medical equipment with a user-friendly digital platform, ensuring that our services are accessible and reliable. Over the years, we have grown significantly, both in terms of our capabilities and the regions we serve.

Cities Where We Serve

Nigeria's premier e-ambulance service, dedicated to providing fast, reliable, and professional emergency medical response across multiple states in the country. We are proud to operate in some of the most populous and diverse regions, ensuring that citizens from urban centers to remote areas have access to critical healthcare services when they need it the most. Our state-of-the-art e-ambulance fleet is actively serving the bustling city of Lagos, Nigeria's economic hub, where we navigate the vibrant streets and neighborhoods to bring timely medical attention to millions of residents.

We also extend our life-saving services to Abia State, known for its rich culture and commerce, ensuring that the people of the southeast are never too far from emergency care.

In the oil-rich state of Rivers, particularly in the capital city of Port Harcourt, we provide vital emergency transport and medical support to residents and workers alike, supporting the state's industrial and commercial heartbeat.

Further north, Adamawa State, with its mountainous terrain and beautiful landscapes, benefits from our e-ambulance services as we strive to reach communities both urban and rural, ensuring no area is too remote for urgent medical assistance.

In Kaduna State, one of Nigeria's largest and most politically significant regions, our team is dedicated to providing rapid response to its diverse population, bridging gaps in medical emergency services across the state's expansive geography.

Similarly, in Katsina State, where health services can be spread thin across rural and suburban areas, Life Link steps in to offer reliable, quick access to emergency care, delivering aid to those in need in record time.

Finally, in the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), Abuja, our e-ambulance services are available around the clock, ensuring that residents, government officials, and visitors in the nation's capital have a trusted emergency medical service ready to respond at a moment's notice.

At Life Link, we are committed to expanding our reach and continually improving our services to better serve the people of Nigeria, ensuring that no matter where you are—whether in the thriving metropolis of Lagos or the tranquil landscapes of Adamawa—you can rely on us for your emergency medical needs.
